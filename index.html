About Gabby

From college kitchens to
your celebration table

Gabby trained her craft through hands-on baking classes at college, where a class project turned into a genuine obsession with sugar work, laminated layers and getting buttercream perfectly smooth. Every cake on this site started as coursework and grew into something she's proud to put her name on.

She bakes everything herself, in small batches, so every order gets proper attention — no two cakes come out quite the same.
