About me

From college kitchens to
your celebration table

I trained my craft through hands-on baking classes at college, where a class project turned into a genuine obsession with sugar work, laminated layers and getting buttercream perfectly smooth. Every cake on this site started as coursework and grew into something I'm proud to put my name on.

I bake everything myself, in small batches, so every order gets proper attention — no two cakes come out quite the same.
